Projects

Here are the projects I have worked on in the last two years. I have focused on solving kaggle projects due to the access to data.

Jane Street Market Prediction Kaggle Competition 2021 (Ongoing)

Developed a Deep Learning Model to predict whether to take a trading opportunity or not. Created a custom loss function based on the results of the trade opportunity provide.
The Jane Street Market Prediction is a binary class prediction problem, with a custom reward/loss if the trade was taken.
To do better on this problem I plan on setting this up as a counterfactual learning problem with 2 actions and a uniform policy distribution (since we know both actions)
Am currently working on developing the framework for the counterfactual learning model I am to develop for this dataset.

Mechanism of Action (MoA) Multi-Classification Kaggle Competition (2020)

Developing a model to predict the mechanism of action for a disease given the cell data and response to a drug.
The mechanism of action is a multiclass classification problem, with a mix of continuous and discrete data provided.
Designed a model involving the use of a NN model with 15 hidden layers using a Tanh activation function and a SoftMax function on the final layer, that is trained using mini-batch gradient descent.
Applied PCA to reduce the dimension till 99% accuracy, before using it in the model. Reached a final score of 0.982.

SIIM-ISIC Melanoma Classification Kaggle Competition (2020)

Developing a model that uses images of skin lesions to predict whether or not the image represents a melanoma.
Utilized multiple layers of filters to try and extract useful information and then prepared a NN implementation with 25 layers with ReLu activation units and a sigmoid activation function at the end, using a logarithmic loss to optimize.
Reached a score of 0.936 on the final competition.
